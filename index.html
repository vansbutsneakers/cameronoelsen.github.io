Join and contribute to
live collaborative playlysts

No need to switch the AUX

There’s no need to switch the AUX cord anymore. Within seconds, be connected to a nearby playlyst and contribute to the mix easily from your own device.

Make music social with song voting

Get a feel for what everyone wants to hear with live song voting. Playlyst intelligently knows what song wants to be heard sooner, and will automatically place it next in the queue.

Share your playlist effortlessly

Playlyst works over the cloud, but that doesn’t mean sharing it has to be complicated. Share your playlyst over bluetooth, text, or social media.

Keep it private

We get it, there are times where we’d like to keep the music contribution limited to only a few hosts. With Playlyst, you can create a private Playlyst, only allowing access to the very few who know the password
