Frequently Asked Questions

What is Playlyst?

Playlyst eliminates the need to switch the aux cable, connect multiple devices to bluetooth, and fight over the music choice at any event. Simply connect one device to the sound system, and have everyone join the playlyst from their phones. From there, they can contribute by adding their own music from various sources like SoundCloud, Apple Music, Spotify, and their own music library. Once a playlyst is made, users can also vote songs up and down to let playlyst know what they want to hear. Songs will play in order of popularity. Let your sound travel.

Why don't I see all music services?

Playlyst works with the music services linked to the host device. In other words, if the host device has a Spotify, Apple Music, or SoundCloud account linked to it, then users can contribute songs from those services – without being subscribed. The chart below might simplify things

Service Account Needed to Contribute
Apple Music NO
Spotify NO
SoundCloud YES
Music Library NO

How do I vote songs up and down?

Voting songs is easy, simply tap the arrows on the right of the song name. Up if you want to hear the song, down if it’s not really your jam at the moment. You can’t vote on songs that are currently playing

How can I share my Playlyst ID?

You can share your Playlyst ID in many ways. Simply tap the Playlyst ID at the top of your playlyst. You’ll then be greeted with a scannable QR code. Additionally, you can tap on the button above the QR code to distribute your Playlyst ID over text, email, twitter, or facebook.

Can I share my Playlyst over bluetooth?

Yes, the host device can share their Playlyst over bluetooth. Everyone in range will be able to detect and join the playlyst. You are not, however, tethered to the host device over bluetooth – Meaning once you join, you can walk away or leave – although we’re not quite sure why you’d want to leave the party.

How can I create a private Playlyst?

We get it, sometimes you just don’t want everyone to contribute to the music choice. That’s where your Playlyst password comes into play. You can set a Playlyst Password as you’re creating the playlyst. If you decide you’d like to add a password to the playlyst after you’ve created it, you can do so in your playlyst settings. Just tap the button in the top left corner if you’re the host. Everyone will be required to use a password to join and contribute to the playlyst
